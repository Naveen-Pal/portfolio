## Game Overview

Void Battle is an immersive text-based space combat game that puts players in command of an ISRO-developed spacecraft. In this thrilling adventure, Earth faces an unprecedented alien invasion, and you are humanity's last line of defense.

## Story

In the near future, Earth comes under attack from a massive alien fleet. As civilization faces its greatest threat, ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation) develops an advanced combat spacecraft. As the chosen pilot, you must engage in intense battles against alien ships to protect Earth from invasion.

## Features

- Terminal-based gameplay
- Strategic combat mechanics
- Progressive difficulty
- Text-based visualization
- Command-line interface

## Development

This game was created as part of the Technical Council Competition, demonstrating the potential of terminal-based gaming and creative storytelling through simple yet engaging mechanics.

Try it yourself by cloning the repository and joining the battle to save Earth! 🚀
